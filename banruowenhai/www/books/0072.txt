<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-align:center"><b><span style="font-size:36.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt;color:#FF6600">妙法莲华经</span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-align:center"><b><span style="font-size:36.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt;color:#FF6600">观世音菩萨普门品<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-align:center"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-align:center"><span style="color:#003300">姚秦三藏法师鸠摩罗什译<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">尔时，无尽意菩萨即从座起，偏袒右肩，合掌向佛而作是言：‘世尊！观世音菩萨以何因缘，名观世音？’佛告无尽意菩萨：‘善男子！若有无量百千万亿众生受诸苦恼，闻是观世音菩萨，一心称名，观世音菩萨即时观其音声，皆得解脱。若有持是观世音菩萨名者，设入大火，火不能烧，由是菩萨威神力故；若为大水所漂，称其名号，即得浅处。若有百千万亿众生，为求金、银、琉璃、砗磲、玛瑙、珊瑚、琥珀、真珠等宝，入于大海，假使黑风吹其船舫，飘堕罗刹鬼国，其中若有乃至一人称观世音菩萨名者，是诸人等皆得解脱罗刹之难，以是因缘，名观世音。’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">‘若复有人临当被害，称观世音菩萨名者，彼所执刀杖寻段段坏，而得解脱。若三千大千国土满中夜叉罗刹，欲来恼人，闻其称观世音菩萨名者，是诸恶鬼尚不能以恶眼视之，况复加害？设复有人，若有罪、若无罪，杻械、枷锁检系其身，称观世音菩萨名者，皆悉断坏，即得解脱。若三千大千国土满中怨贼，有一商主将诸商人赍持重宝，经过险路，其中一人作是唱言：“诸善男子！勿得恐怖，汝等应当一心称观世音菩萨名号，是菩萨能以无畏施于众生。汝等若称名者，于此怨贼当得解脱。”众商人闻，俱发声言：“南无观世音菩萨！”称其名故，即得解脱。’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoBodyTextIndent" style="margin-right:-5.14gd">‘无尽意！观世音菩萨摩诃萨威神之力，巍巍如是。若有众生多于淫欲，常念恭敬观世音菩萨，便得离欲；若多嗔恚，常念恭敬观世音菩萨，便得离嗔；若多愚痴，常念恭敬观世音菩萨，便得离痴。无尽意！观世音菩萨有如是等大威神力，多所饶益，是故众生常应心念。若有女人设欲求男，礼拜供养观世音菩萨，便生福德智慧之男；设欲求女，便生端正有相之女，宿植德本，众人爱敬。无尽意！观世音菩萨有如是力，若有众生恭敬礼拜观世音菩萨，福不唐捐；是故众生皆应受持观世音菩萨名号。’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">‘无尽意！<span style="mso-bidi-font-weight:bold">若有人受持六十二亿恒河沙菩萨名字，复尽形供养饮食、衣服、卧具、医药。于汝意云何？是善男子、善女人功德多不？</span>’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">无尽意言：‘甚多！世尊！’佛言：‘若复有人受持观世音菩萨名号，乃至一时礼拜供养，是二人福正等无异，于百千万亿劫不可穷尽。无尽意！受持观世音菩萨名号，得如是无量无边福德之利。’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoBodyTextIndent2">无尽意菩萨白佛言：‘世尊！观世音菩萨云何游此娑婆世界？云何而为众生说法？方便之力，其事云何？’佛告无尽意菩萨：‘善男子！若有国土众生应以佛身得度者，观世音菩萨即现佛身而为说法；应以辟支佛身得度者，即现辟支佛身而为说法；应以声闻身得度者，即现声闻身而为说法；应以梵王身得度者，即现梵王身而为说法；应以帝释身得度者，即现帝释身而为说法；应以自在天身得度者，即现自在天身而为说法；应以大自在天身得度者，即现大自在天身而为说法；应以天大将军身得度者，即现天大将军身而为说法；应以毗沙门身得度者，即现毗沙门身而为说法；应以小王身得度者，即现小王身而为说法；应以长者身得度者，即现长者身而为说法；应以居士身得度者，即现居士身而为说法；应以宰官身得度者，即现宰官身而为说法；应以婆罗门身得度者，即现婆罗门身而为说法；应以比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷身得度者，即现比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷身而为说法；应以长者、居士、宰官、婆罗门妇女身得度者，即现妇女身而为说法；应以童男、童女身得度者，即现童男、童女身而为说法，应以天、龙、夜叉、乾闼婆、阿修罗、迦楼罗、紧那罗、摩 [目*侯] 罗伽、人非人等身得度者，即皆现之而为说法；应以执金刚神得度者，即现执金刚神而为说法。’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">‘无尽意！是观世音菩萨成就如是功德，以种种形，游诸国土，度脱众生，是故汝等应当一心供养观世音菩萨。是观世音菩萨摩诃萨于怖畏急难之中，能施无畏，是故，此娑婆世界皆号之为施无畏者。’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">无尽意菩萨白佛言：‘世尊！我今当供养观世音菩萨。’即解颈众宝珠璎珞，价值百千两金，而以与之，作是言：‘仁者！受此法施珍宝璎珞。’时，观世音菩萨不肯受之。无尽意复白观世音菩萨言：‘仁者！愍我等故，受此璎珞。’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">尔时，佛告观世音菩萨：‘当愍此无尽意菩萨及四众，天、龙、夜叉、乾闼婆、阿修罗、迦楼罗、紧那罗、摩 [目*侯] 罗伽、人非人等故，受是璎珞。’即时，观世音菩萨愍诸四众及于天、龙、人非人等，受其璎珞；分作二分，一分奉释迦牟尼佛，一分奉多宝佛塔。‘无尽意！观世音菩萨有如是自在神力，游于娑婆世界。’</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">尔时，无尽意菩萨以偈问曰：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">世尊妙相具<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>我今重问彼<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>佛子何因缘<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>名为观世音</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">具足妙相尊<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>偈答无尽意<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>汝听观音行<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>善应诸方所</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">弘誓深如海<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>历劫不思议<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>侍多千亿佛<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>发大清净愿</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我为汝略说<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>闻名及见身<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>心念不空过<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>能灭诸有苦</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">假使兴害意<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>推落大火坑<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>火坑变成池</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">或漂流巨海<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>龙鱼诸鬼难<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>波浪不能没</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">或在须弥峰<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>为人所推堕<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>如日虚空住</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">或被恶人逐<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>堕落金刚山<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>不能损一毛</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">或值怨贼绕<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>各执刀加害<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>咸即起慈心</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">或遭王难苦<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>临刑欲寿终<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>刀寻段段坏</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">或囚禁枷锁<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>手足被杻械<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>释然得解脱</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">咒诅诸毒药<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>所欲害身者<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>还著于本人</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">或遇恶罗刹<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>毒龙诸鬼等<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>时悉不敢害 </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">若恶兽围绕<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>利牙爪可怖<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>疾走无边方</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">蚖蛇及蝮蝎<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>气毒烟火然<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>寻声自回去</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">云雷鼓掣电<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>降雹澍大雨<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>应时得消散</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">众生被困厄<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>无量苦逼身<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>观音妙智力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>能救世间苦</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">具足神通力<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>广修智方便<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>十方诸国土<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>无刹不现身</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">种种诸恶趣<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>地狱鬼畜生<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>生老病死苦 <span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;</span>以渐悉令灭</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">真观清净观<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>广大智慧观<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>悲观及慈观<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>常愿常瞻仰</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">无垢清净光<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>慧日破诸闇<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>能伏灾风火<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>普明照世间 </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">悲体戒雷震<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>慈意妙大云<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>澍甘露法雨<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>灭除烦恼焰</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">诤讼经官处<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>怖畏军阵中<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>念彼观音力<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>众怨悉退散</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">妙音观世音<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>梵音海潮音<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>胜彼世间音<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>是故须常念</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">念念勿生疑<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>观世音净圣<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>于苦恼死厄<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>能为作依怙</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-right:-5.14gd;text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">尔时，持地菩萨即从座起，前白佛言：‘世尊！若有众生闻是观世音菩萨品自在之业，普门示现神通力者，当知是人功德不少。’佛说是普门品时，众中八万四千众生皆发无等等阿耨多罗三藐三菩提心。</p>
