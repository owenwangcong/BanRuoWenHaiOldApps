<p><span style="color:#333399">［十九］为什么接佛后，又翻掌平贴地？<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333399">一、佛法修心方面的意义：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-US">1、接佛后，又轻轻‘半握拳’，代表领纳佛光，领受佛教，欢喜信受。</span></p>
<p><span style="color:olive">也如蕅益大师云：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-US" style="color:olive">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color:olive">‘行人信愿持名，全摄佛功德成自功德。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-US" style="color:olive">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color:olive">握拳表‘全摄’—全部领受在握，入心。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
