<p align="center" style="text-align:center"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334207"></span><a href="#a10"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334207">十、正释经文</span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334207"></span></a><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334207"></span></p>
<p align="center" style="text-align:center"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334207"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p class="1" style="line-height:normal"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334207"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;color:#333399;mso-font-width:100%"><a name="a00"></a>壹、前　言</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;color:#333399;mso-font-width:100%"></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、此次讲述参考资料：依李炳南老居士提要，及黄念祖老居士注解。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、确认净土法门为一乘了义，万善同归，三根普被，凡圣齐收，横出三界，迳登四土，极圆极顿，不可思议之微妙法门。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三、本经持名念佛法门，圆满直捷，方便究竟。一超直入，最极圆顿。以弥陀一乘愿海，六字洪名之究竟果觉，作我众生之因心，以果为因，因果同时，从果起修，即修即果，心作心是，不可思议。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">四、《弥陀要解》云：‘一声阿弥陀佛，即是释迦本师于五浊恶世所得之阿耨多罗三藐三菩提法。今以此果觉全体授与浊恶众生，乃诸佛所行境界，唯佛与佛能究竟，非九界自力所能信解也。’又曰：‘举此体（指法界体）作弥陀身土，亦即举此体作弥陀名号。是故弥陀名号即众生本觉理性’。可见此经正是如来称性极谈也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">五、《观经》云：‘是心是佛，是心作佛。’吾人能念之心，即是如来果觉，即是本来成佛。此心持名，即是‘是心作佛’。本来是佛，现又作佛，是故当下即佛。直截了当，方便究竟，奇特殊胜，不可思议。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">六、净土诸经中，唯此经备摄圆妙，以发菩提心，一向专念为宗。以弥陀十念必生之大愿为本。深明三辈往生之因，广摄九界圣凡之众，正显持名念佛之法，直指往生归元之路，是故此经称为净宗第一经也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">七、清初彭绍升居士曰：‘此经阐扬者少，实以无善本故。’莲公老人悲智双运，宗说俱通，圆融显密禅净于一心，专弘持名念佛摄万德。冀此无上宝典，饶益当来，乃继前贤，重行会集。屏弃万缘，掩关三载，净坛结界，冥心孤诣，稿经十易方庆经成。蒙慧明老和尚印证，慈舟专讲于济南，并亲为科判。梅公于中广播讲此经，称为最善之本。且在序文中赞曰：‘精当明确，凿然有据。无一义不在原译之中，无一句溢出本经之外。艰涩沉晦，使之爽朗；繁复冗蔓，归于简洁；凌乱俾成整严，阙疏悉令圆满。必期有美皆备，无谛不收。……虽欲不谓之善本不可得也。’是此本问世以来，讲说赞扬，流播中外，见者闻者，欢喜信受，行见大经光明，常照世间。《无量寿经》之善本，于兹庆现，此实为希有难逢之大事因缘也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">八、本经之殊胜在于契理契机。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>理者：实际理体，亦即真如实相。本经乃‘住真实慧’，‘开化显示真实之际’，并‘惠以真实之利’，纯一真实也。</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>又本经称为《中本华严经》，经中所诠之一切事理，即《华严》之事理无碍，事事无碍之一真法界。《华严》秘奥之理体，却在本经。</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>机者：根机。至于契机，更是本经之独胜。以持名念佛，三根普被，凡圣齐收，上上根全体承当，下下根亦可依之得度。下至五逆十恶，临终念佛，亦必随愿得生。横出三界，圆登四土，顿与观音势至并肩。可见此法门究竟方便，善应群机也。</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">九、净宗之妙，在于‘不离佛法，而行世法；不废世法，而证佛法’。最为方便，随时随地可修，不误世间工作，依旧顿脱生死。既能自觉觉他，广度众生于未来，亦复自他俱利，造福社会于今世。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">十、经云：‘当来之世，经道灭尽，我以慈悲哀愍，特留此经止住百岁。其有众生，值斯经者，随意所愿，皆可得度。’诚此界他方一切有情，离苦得乐，究竟菩提之法要也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="1" style="line-height:normal"><a name="_Toc388334208"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;color:#333399;mso-font-width:100%">贰、概要</span></a><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334208"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;color:#333399;letter-spacing:0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（十门开启—玄义）</span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334208"></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;color:#333399;mso-font-width:100%"></span></p>

<p class="2" style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:1.45;mso-char-indent-size:12.4pt;line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">六、藏教所摄 七、部类差别 八、译会校释 九、总释名题 十、正释经文<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="2" style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:1.45;mso-char-indent-size:12.4pt;line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Toc388334209"></a><a name="a01"></a><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334209"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334209"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">一、教起因缘</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent:27.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.25;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、明大教与净土法门之兴起。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l39 level1 lfo1"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一切法不离因缘，大教之兴，因缘无量，而无量因缘，唯为一大事因缘故。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l39 level1 lfo1"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《法华》云：‘诸佛世尊唯以一大事因缘故，出现于世。’《华严》云：‘如来成等正觉出兴于世，以十种无量无数因缘。……乃至广说如是等无量因缘，唯为一大事因缘。……唯欲众生开示佛知见故。’亦即欲一切众生开明本心，同佛知见，等成正觉也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l39 level1 lfo1"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">本经兴起因缘亦复如是。经云：‘我观如是利益安乐大事因缘，说诚谛语。’可见与《华严》、《法华》同为一大事因缘也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l39 level1 lfo1"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《疏钞》云：‘今但一心持名，即得不退。此乃直指凡夫自心究竟成佛。若能谛信，何须遍历三乘，久经多劫。不越一念，顿证菩提，岂非大事。’可见净宗正是直指顿超之法，以念佛心，入佛知见。净宗之兴起，正由此大事因缘也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:1.0cm;text-indent:0cm;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="margin-top:6.0pt;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:6.0pt;margin-left:1.0cm;line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、详明本经兴起之因缘。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l7 level1 lfo2"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">称性极谈，如来正说。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l23 level1 lfo3"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《华严》、《法华》，均圆顿称性之教，但其归趣，却在本经。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l23 level1 lfo3"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《要解》云：‘绝待圆融，不可思议，《华严》奥藏，《法华》秘髓，一切诸佛之心要，菩萨万行之司南，皆不出于此矣。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l23 level1 lfo3"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">日道隐曰：‘五浊之世，造恶之时，圣道难修，其难非一。特此一门，至圆极顿，而且由其简易直捷，则出世之正说偏在斯经。一代所说归此经，如众水归于大海。由此言之，百万阿僧祇因缘以起华严之典，一大事因缘以成法华之教。亦唯为此法之由序。’盖谓《华严》，《法华》两经只是本经之导引，本经正是一大藏教之指归。《华严》末后十大愿王，导归极乐，是其明证。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l23 level1 lfo3"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">４、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《楞严》云：‘忆佛念佛，现前当来，必定见佛。’‘净念相继’、‘自得心开。’奇妙疾捷，莫过于是。一切众生由此得度，十方如来乃称本怀。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l23 level1 lfo3"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">５、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">本经所宣，乃如来真实纯一之法，无有权曲，故名正说。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l23 level1 lfo3"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">６、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">世尊称其本性，和盘托出，毫无保留，故名称性。一切含灵，皆因此而得度，乃称性中登峰造极之谈，故为称性极谈。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l45 level1 lfo4"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三根普被，圣凡齐收。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l43 level1 lfo5"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">彻上，则如普贤、文殊，尚发愿求生极乐。彻下，则五逆十恶，临终得遇善友，教以念佛，十念成功，亦生彼国。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l43 level1 lfo5"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">本经云：‘值斯经者，随意所愿，皆可得度。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l43 level1 lfo5"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">末法众生，福薄障深，唯此一门，但信愿持名，便能功超累劫，往生极乐，径登不退。若无如是微妙法门，凡夫何能度此生死业海，而登彼岸。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l43 level1 lfo5"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">４、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">元晓师云：‘四十八愿，先为凡夫，兼为三乘圣人。’可见净土之妙，首为凡夫得度也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l40 level1 lfo6"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">他力妙法，善护行人。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l2 level1 lfo7"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">末世修行，多诸障难，阴魔干扰，行人正见稍失，便陷魔网。《楞严》曰：‘不作圣心，名善境界；若作圣解，即受群邪。’可见行人稍有执著，便失正见，即入魔途，求升反降，甚至堕入泥犁。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l2 level1 lfo7"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">念佛法门，行人发心念佛，仗弥陀本愿摄受，威神加持，护持行者，魔不能扰。《十往生经》云：‘若有众生念阿弥陀佛，愿往生者，彼佛即遣二十五菩萨，拥护行者。一切时，一切处，不令恶鬼恶神得其便也’。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l2 level1 lfo7"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《弥陀经》云：‘若有善男子、善女人，闻是经受持者，及闻诸佛名者。是诸善男子、善女人，皆为一切诸佛之所护念。’是故念佛人有四十里光明烛身，魔不能犯。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l41 level1 lfo8"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">暗合道妙，巧入无生。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l44 level1 lfo9"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">‘无念’与‘无生’，远非凡夫之所能。圆教八地菩萨，离一切心意识分别，始名真得无生法忍。可见此乃圣者之所难也。如来悬知末代罪浊众生，立相住心尚不能得，何况离相而求事者。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l44 level1 lfo9"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">此方便门，指方立相，摄心专注，即此妄心，持佛名号，念一佛名，换除百千万亿妄想杂念。念得纯熟，乃忘能所。心无所住，佛号分明，便契无住生心之妙谛。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l44 level1 lfo9"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">持名念佛，入有得空，潜通佛智，暗合道妙，巧入无念，即凡成圣。至哉妙用，不可思议。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l44 level1 lfo9"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">４、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">念佛乃修行之径路，持名又为四种念佛之径路，称为径中之径。又本经不倡‘一心不乱’，直以‘发菩提心，一向专念阿弥陀佛’为宗。‘但能发心专念，皆可往生’，故更为明确简要。故称净宗第一经。因其为直捷中之直捷，方便中之方便，绝待圆融，不可思议。利济众生，此经为最。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l26 level1 lfo10"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(五)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">大圣垂慈，特留此经。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l49 level1 lfo11"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">经云：‘当来之世，经道灭尽。我以慈悲哀愍，特留此经，止住百岁。其有众生，值斯经者，随意所愿，皆可得度。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l49 level1 lfo11"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">今于末世，浊恶弥深，众根愈劣，垢重障深，恶浪滔天，毒焰遍地，世尊垂慈，仍特留此经以作慈航。正显本经兴起之胜缘，持名法门之妙用。佛恩深重，粉身难报。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Toc388334210"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334210"><a name="a02"></a><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334210"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">二、本经体性</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、一切大乘经典，皆以实相为体。实相者，真实相也，又平等一相也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>实相无相—离一切虚妄相。‘凡所有相，皆是虚妄’。</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>实相无不相—非顽空与断灭。‘若见诸相非相，即见如来’。</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、实相离言说。南岳云：‘说似一物即不中。’故云，凡有言说，皆无实义。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三、《圆觉》云：‘诸幻尽灭，觉心不动。’‘幻灭灭故，非幻不灭。’故云，不用求真，唯须息见。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">四、离一切相，即一切法。离故无相，即故无不相。不得已强名实相。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">五、论云：‘庄严佛土功德成就，庄严佛、菩萨、功德成就，此三种成就愿心庄严应知。略说入一法句故；一法句者，谓清净句。清净句者，谓真实智慧无为法身故。’此论直显事理无碍之一真法界。即事而真，当相即道。极乐世界种种依正庄严，一一皆是真实智慧无为法身，一一即是实相。故云本经以实相为体也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">六、《要解》云：‘实相无二，亦无不二。是故举体作依作正，作法作报，作自作他。乃至能说所说，能度所度，能信所信，能愿所愿，能持所持，能生所生，能赞所赞，无非实相正印之所印也。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">七、本经云：‘开化显示真实之际’。‘欲拯群萌，惠以真实之利’。‘住真实慧，勇猛精进，一向专志庄严妙土’。此即本经之所开示者。弥陀世尊，宣此妙法，欲惠予众生以真实之利也。此三真实，即一即三，方便究竟，不可思议。依正法门，举体皆是‘真实之际’。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Toc388334211"></a><a name="a03"></a><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334211"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334211"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">三、一经宗趣</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、宗谓宗旨，又修行之要径也。趣者，趣向、归趣也。体是理，宗是行。是故依经宗旨，明其所为，识其所求，究其所至，名之为趣。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、本经以‘发菩提心，一向专念’为宗。以‘圆生四土，迳登不退’为趣。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三、明宗：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l31 level1 lfo12"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">经云：三辈往生之人，莫不以‘发菩提心，一向专念’为因。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l31 level1 lfo12"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">第十九愿是发菩提心，第十八愿是一向专念。故知‘发菩提心，一向专念’，是弥陀本愿之心髓，全部大经之宗要，往生必修之正因，方便度生之慈航。本经所崇，全在于此。修行要径，唯斯捷要，故为本经之宗也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l31 level1 lfo12"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">发心有二义，先须离三种与菩提门相违法。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l16 level1 lfo13"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">依智慧门，不求自乐，远离我心贪著自身故。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l16 level1 lfo13"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">依慈悲门，拔一切众生苦，远离无安众生心故。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l16 level1 lfo13"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">依方便门，怜愍一切众生心，远离恭敬供养自身心故。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>次须知三种顺菩提门：</span></p>
<p class="3" style="margin-left:62.35pt;text-indent:18.65pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:l9 level1 lfo49"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">（1）<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">无染清净心。不为自身求安乐故，菩提是无染清净处。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="text-indent:-4.05pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:l9 level1 lfo49"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">（2）<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">安清净心。为拔一切众生苦故。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="text-indent:-4.05pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:l9 level1 lfo49"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">（3）<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">乐清净心。欲令一切众生得大菩提，生彼国土故。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l24 level1 lfo15"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">元晓师云：‘发菩提心，是明正因。’又云发心有二：<span class="a0"><span style="mso-ansi-font-size:12.0pt;mso-fareast-letter-spacing:.4pt">（１）</span></span>随事发心，如四弘誓愿。<span class="a0"><span style="mso-ansi-font-size:12.0pt;mso-fareast-letter-spacing:.4pt">（２）</span></span>顺理发心，信解诸法实相而发心者。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l24 level1 lfo15"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(五)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">元晓师等以发菩提心为正因，念佛为助行。善导、灵芝与日净宗诸家则以持名为正行，发心为助行。莲池大师和会之曰：‘还以持名为正行，复以持名为发菩提心。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">彻悟禅师云：‘真为生死，发菩提心。以深信愿，持佛名号。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">蕅益大师云：‘信、愿、持名，为一经要旨。信愿为慧行，持名为行行。得生与否，全由信愿之有无；品位高下，全由持名之深浅。故慧行为先导，行行为正修，如目足并运也。’又曰：‘深信发愿，即无上菩提。合此信愿，的为净土指南。由此而执持名号，乃为正行。大本亦以发菩提愿为要，正与此同。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>故今以‘发菩提心，一向专念’为大本之宗，正是遵奉莲池诸祖之垂教也。</span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l10 level1 lfo16"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(六)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《观经》云：‘若有众生，愿生彼国，发三种心，即便往生。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>

<p class="20" style="margin-top:9.0pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:l19 level1 lfo17"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(七)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《菩提心义》云：‘此菩提之心，成佛之本，大事因缘莫过于此。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《菩提心论》云：‘此菩提心，能包藏一切诸佛功德法故。若修证出现，则为一切导师。若归本则是密严土，不起于座，能成一切佛事。’又赞菩提心曰：‘若人求佛慧，通达菩提心，父母所生身，速证大觉位。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l19 level1 lfo17"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(八)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《华严经》云：‘忘失菩提心，修诸善法，是名魔业。’故发心之要，不可言喻。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l19 level1 lfo17"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(九)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">‘一向专念’，即专恒依止持名念佛法门之义也。弥陀因中发十念必生之大愿，故行人发菩提心一向专念，必得往生净土，圆证不退之妙果。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l19 level1 lfo17"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(十)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一向专念者，古云：‘上尽形寿，下至十念。’上尽形寿，指从发心念佛之日，终身念佛，直至命终之最后一念也。</span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">下者，未能及此，或因障重，或因事繁，未暇多念，则于每日行十念法，亦符于‘一向专念’。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">更下，则如《观经》所说恶逆之人，临欲命终，得聆圣教，至心悔改，十念称名，亦得往生，故云十念必生也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">行者应知，一向专念，指从初发心念佛，直至最后一念也。而其关键实在最后。反之，如有人念佛数十年，或于最后阶段，轻视持名而改行他法，或于临终不欣极乐，而恋世间，未能念佛，是则不名一向专念也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:14.2pt;text-indent:0cm;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（十一）</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">或疑念佛何以有如是功德？因能念所念，皆是实相故。</span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《要解》云：‘无量光则横遍十方，无量寿则竖穷三际，横竖交彻皆法界体。举此体作弥陀身土。亦即举此体作弥陀名号。是故弥陀名号，即众生本觉理性。持名即始觉合本，始本不二，生佛不二。故一念相应一念佛，念念相应念念佛也。’故名号功德不可思议。起心念佛，是为始觉。所念之佛，正我本觉。正显持名功德不可思议也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:53.9pt;text-indent:-39.7pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（十二）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一句弥陀，人人能念，个个可行，名召万德，妙感难思，从有念巧入无念，即凡心顿显果德。如《疏钞》云：‘齐诸圣于片言，越三祇于一念。’极圆极顿，至简至易。故知‘发菩提心，一向专念’，不但为本经之纲宗，实亦为一大藏教之指归也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:53.9pt;text-indent:-39.7pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">四、明趣：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l50 level1 lfo18"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">‘发菩提心，一向专念’之所归，在于往生极乐净土，证三不退。故本经以‘圆生四土，迳登不退’为趣。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l50 level1 lfo18"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">略明四土往生之相：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l29 level1 lfo19"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">凡圣同居土—《要解》云，此土凡圣之同居，逊于极乐者有四。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="4" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo20"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:华康细圆体;mso-font-width:100%">(１)</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">暂同。二乘证果便入灭，大权菩萨机尽则不示现。彼土同居，则与诸上善人俱会一处，直至成佛。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="4" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo20"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:华康细圆体;mso-font-width:100%">(２)</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">难遇。虽有圣者，现居此土，不易见闻亲近。彼土则皆如师友，朝夕同聚。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="4" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo20"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:华康细圆体;mso-font-width:100%">(３)</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">希少。圣者如珍如瑞，希有难逢。彼土则‘其中多有一生补处，其数甚多，非是算数所能知之，但可以无量无边阿僧祇说’。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="4" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo20"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:华康细圆体;mso-font-width:100%">(４)</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">所作不同。此土众生轮转六道，升沉无定。彼土则同尽无明，同登妙觉。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="4" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">尤胜者，不待断惑，但能发菩提心，一向专念，便蒙佛愿冥加，决得往生净土。其事甚易，如是方便直捷，殊胜希有，乃十方世界所无。又带业往生，仍属凡夫，但无退转，一生成佛。故往生同居，亦即圆生四土也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l18 level1 lfo21"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">方便有余土—念佛功深，离杂乱心，专念一句名号，心口相应，字字分明。心不离佛，佛不离心，念念相续，无有间断，如是念佛，名事一心。若达此境界，虽不求断惑，而见思烦恼，自然断落，则横生极乐方便有余土。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l18 level1 lfo22"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">实报庄严土—忆佛念佛，历历分明，行住坐卧，唯此一念，无第二念，不为贪嗔痴烦恼诸念之所杂乱，是名事一心。事上即得，理上未彻，属定门摄，未有慧故。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="margin-left:6.0gd;text-indent:-8.6pt;mso-char-indent-count:-.67;mso-char-indent-size:12.8pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">更进一步，于自本性，忽然契合，了知能念所念唯是一心，不住有念，不落无念。如《疏钞》云：‘若言其有，则能念之心，本体自空，所念之佛，了不可得。若言其无，则能念之心，灵灵不昧，所念之佛，历历分明。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="margin-left:6.0gd;text-indent:-8.6pt;mso-char-indent-count:-.67;mso-char-indent-size:12.8pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">无念而念，念而无念。言思路绝，不可名状，唯是一心，清净本然之体。更有何法而得杂乱，是名理一心。属慧门摄，兼得定故。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="margin-left:6.0gd;text-indent:-8.6pt;mso-char-indent-count:-.67;mso-char-indent-size:12.8pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">念佛若达理一心，破一分无明，则生实报庄严土，同时分证常寂光净土。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l18 level1 lfo22"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">４、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">常寂光土—法身佛所居之土，名为常寂光土。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="text-indent:-13.05pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《要解》云：‘实相之体，非寂非照。而复寂而恒照，照而恒寂。照而寂，强名常寂光土。寂而照，强名清净法身。’故知身土不二，皆属强名。寂照同时，非思量境。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>

<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">等觉大士，历尽四十一品无明，更破最后一品生相无明。法身全显，圆证三德。彻本心源，究竟清净，证入常寂光土。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l30 level1 lfo23"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">凡夫带业往生同居土，以亲闻佛训故，无退转故，寿命无量故，故必于此一生，圆断诸惑，圆净四土。故生同居，亦即生上三土，故曰圆生四土。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l30 level1 lfo24"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">极乐胜于十方者，首在带业凡夫，一生彼国，便不退转。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>

<p class="20" style="margin-top:12.0pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:l28 level1 lfo25"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(五)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">此土修证三种不退：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l36 level1 lfo26"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">位不退—藏初果，通见地，别初住，圆初信。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l36 level1 lfo26"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">行不退—通菩萨，别十向，圆十信。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l36 level1 lfo26"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">念不退—别初地，圆初住。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l35 level1 lfo27"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(六)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《要解》云：‘五逆十恶，十念成就，带业往生，居下下品，皆得三不退’。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l35 level1 lfo27"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(七)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">极乐不退之因缘有五：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l32 level1 lfo28"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">弥陀大悲愿力摄持。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l32 level1 lfo28"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">佛光常照，菩提心增进。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l32 level1 lfo28"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">六尘水鸟悉演妙法，增其正念。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l32 level1 lfo28"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">４、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">纯诸菩萨以为胜友，外无魔邪，内无烦恼。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l32 level1 lfo28"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">５、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">寿命永劫，与佛齐等。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l34 level1 lfo29"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(八)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">此凡夫易生之同居净土，行人不须断惑消业，只要信愿持名。下至十念，皆得往生。永离诸苦，位登不退，一生补处，不可思议。是乃十方世界之所无，故为千经万论所同赞者也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l34 level1 lfo30"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(九)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">往生同居，便是横生上三土。证位不退，亦即圆证三不退。念念普利群生，心心流入觉海，是为本经之趣。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Toc388334212"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334212"><a name="a04"></a><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334212"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">四、方便力用</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、度生方便正是诸佛大愿之所结，大智之所显，万德之所庄严，果觉之究竟处也。是故继体性宗趣后，而论本经方便胜妙之大力大用。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、诸佛出世有三种利益：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l22 level1 lfo31"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">说经，法施利益，令众生破迷开悟，早证无上菩提。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l22 level1 lfo31"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">诸佛如来有身相光明，无量相好。若有众生，称念观察，皆能灭罪消业，永背三途。随意所乐，常生净土，乃至成佛。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l22 level1 lfo31"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">劝令父王，行念佛三昧。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:1.0cm;text-indent:0cm;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三、《观佛三昧经》佛告父王：‘一切众生，生死中念佛之心，但能系念不止，定生佛前。一得往生，即能改变一切诸恶，成大慈悲。’此乃世尊大慈至孝，劝父之言，唯劝念佛。可见种种行门，非不殊胜，但非凡众之所能修。唯有系心念佛法门，最是应机。但能依教念佛，定能往生。一得往生，便能转恶成善。念佛之方便妙用显示无余。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">四、念佛有四种：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l33 level1 lfo32"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">持名念佛。即本经所宗，称念‘阿弥陀佛’名号。善导大师曰：‘唯有径路修行，但念阿弥陀佛。’因其方便直捷，成功迅速也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l33 level1 lfo32"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">观像念佛。以供养圣像，注目观视，然像去还无，因成间断。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l33 level1 lfo32"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">观想念佛。如《观经》所说：‘以我心目，想彼如来。’但境细心粗，妙观难成。故不如持名方便易行。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l33 level1 lfo32"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">实相念佛。远离生灭、有无、能所、言说、名字、心缘等相，专念自性本具天真之佛。实相念佛，圆觉之性，虽然众生本具，但因妄心念念生灭，而不能观。故佛告父王，诸佛本德，真如实相等，非是凡夫所行境界，故劝父王持名念佛也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">四种念佛，持名念佛，最为方便究竟，故称为径中之径。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">五、四种念佛，难易悬殊，深浅似异，实则事理不二，浅深相即。若是圆人看来，称名暗合道妙，初步即是到家，故持名念佛又何异于实相念佛。《疏钞》云：‘实相云者，非必灭除诸相，盖即相而无相也。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">六、又持名即是甚深般若。念得纯熟，万缘齐放，能所顿空，即是无住。于此之时，一句佛号，朗然明白，相续不断，即是生心。故暗合道妙，句句是佛知见，念念放般若光，念佛号即是念实相也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">七、小本有‘一心不乱’之文。今本不云‘一心不乱’，而专主‘一向专念’。是所宗更为明确，更契众生根机，弥显慈尊恩德。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">八、弥陀无尽大悲胜愿，方便至极，力用难思，凡圣齐收，利钝俱被。但能发心专念，悉得度脱。如是方称如来本怀，才是究竟方便。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Toc388334213"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334213"><a name="a05"></a><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334213"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">五、所被根机</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、《疏钞》云：‘前三非器，谓无信者，无愿者，无行者。反是皆器。’信谓信生佛不二，是心是佛，是心作佛，众生念佛，定得往生，究竟成佛。愿谓厌离娑婆，欣慕极乐，如子忆母，必欲往生。行谓从愿起行，一向专念，无有间断。以上三事，号为资粮。信愿行三，缺一不可。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、净土法门，奇特殊妙，当机之说，亦有三例：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l17 level1 lfo33"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">上辈往生，唯是菩萨。唐善导大师以前之古德，多认为上品上生者，是四地至七地。上中品是初地至四地。……从上上至中上四品，凡夫绝分。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l17 level1 lfo33"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">本为凡夫，兼为圣人。善导大师《四帖疏》，破前说曰：‘如来说此十六观法，但为常没众生，不干大小圣也。’又云：‘五浊凡夫，但以遇缘有异，致令九品差别。’又云：‘其机者，则一切善恶大小凡愚也。’大师之说，上契圣心，下洽群机，实大有功于净土。故中日诸师，多承此说。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l17 level1 lfo33"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">专接上根，旁及中下。《疏钞》曰：‘《起信因缘分疏》明信位初心，有四种机。以礼忏灭罪被初机，以修习止观被中机，以求生净土被上机。初谓业障众生，中谓凡夫二乘，是知净土是大乘菩萨所修矣。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">又《要解》谓持名‘法门深妙，破尽一切戏论，斩尽一切意见。唯马鸣、龙树、智者、永明之流，彻底担荷得去。’因持名一法，乃至圆至顿，无上法门，非思量分别之所能及，故曰深妙。但蓦直念去，不假方便，不落思量，直起直用，自得心开。当下破尽戏论，荡除一切计度分别。是能直下承当者，肉身大士也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三、《要解》云：‘故一声阿弥陀佛，即释迦本师于五浊恶世所得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提法。今以此果觉全体，授与浊恶众生，乃诸佛所行境界，唯佛与佛能究竟，非九界自力所能信解也。’可见念佛法门，实是无上深妙之法，唯有上根方能直下承当也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">四、《疏钞》云：‘故知念佛，菩萨之父，生育法身。乃至十地，始终不离念佛。何得初心自足，不愿往生。’可见净土法门，正契菩萨之机。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">五、盖此法门，乃弥陀住真实慧，从真实之际之所开化显示。故唯佛与佛方能究竟。弥陀一乘愿海，六字洪名，圆融具德，超情离见，举体是事理无碍、事事无碍之一真法界，故不可思议也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">六、行人若能于此无上甚深微妙之法，能稍生信解，其人必是上根。如来从真实慧中，开演此法门者，欲惠众生真实之利也。故此法门必方便究竟，普被三根，普令现在、当来，一切凡、愚、浊恶众生，悉登彼岸。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">七、大愿之核心乃第十八愿。然十念必生之究竟方便，实由于弥陀究竟成就之最极方便处，正是本经中‘如来定慧究畅无极’处。今人焉能因此法之易行而鄙视之，而轻此法门乎。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">八、《疏钞》云：‘但持佛名，必生彼国。则或高或下，或圣或凡，乃至或信或疑，或赞或毁，知有彼佛，便成善根。多劫多生，俱蒙解脱。’至于当世，何人当机。则不论男女老少，富贵贫贱，上智下愚，久修初习，宿根利钝，善恶差别。只要于此法门，能生实信，因信发愿，从愿起行。‘发菩提心，一向专念’，如是之人，正是当机。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Toc388334214"></a><a name="a06"></a><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334214"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334214"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">六、藏教所摄</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、凡体究经典，宜先知此经属于何藏，说在何时，判属何教。知此，则有助于理解全经。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、经典能包含蕴积文义，故名为藏。有二：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l11 level1 lfo34"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三藏：<span class="a0"><span style="mso-ansi-font-size:12.0pt;mso-fareast-font-family:细明体;letter-spacing:.4pt">（１）</span></span>契经<span class="a0"><span style="mso-ansi-font-size:12.0pt;mso-fareast-letter-spacing:.4pt">（２）</span></span>律<span class="a0"><span style="mso-ansi-font-size:12.0pt;mso-fareast-letter-spacing:.4pt">（３）</span></span>论。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>本经属经藏。</span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l11 level1 lfo34"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二藏：<span class="a0"><span style="mso-ansi-font-size:12.0pt;mso-fareast-font-family:细明体;letter-spacing:.4pt">（１）</span></span>声闻藏<span class="a0"><span style="mso-ansi-font-size:12.0pt;mso-fareast-letter-spacing:.4pt">（２）</span></span>菩萨藏。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>本经广演大乘，详陈信愿往生，殊胜因果，净佛国土，依正庄严，弥陀无边愿海，普度众生之道。故为菩萨藏摄。</span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:1.0cm;text-indent:0cm;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三、说时有五：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l12 level1 lfo35"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">华严时：最初三七日说。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l12 level1 lfo35"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">阿含时：次十二年说。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l12 level1 lfo35"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">方等时：次八年说，广演四教，均被众机。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l12 level1 lfo35"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">般若时：次二十二年说。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l12 level1 lfo35"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(五)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">法华涅槃时：后八年说法华，一日一夜说涅槃。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">说此法时，在第三方等时。又此经蒙世尊多次宣说，故不应限于一时也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">四、判教：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l4 level1 lfo36"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">天台宗立四教：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（１）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">藏教</span><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（２）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">通教</span><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（３）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">别教</span><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（４）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">圆教。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l4 level1 lfo36"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">贤首宗立五教：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（１）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">小乘教</span><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（２）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">大乘始教</span><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（３）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">大乘终教</span><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（４）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">顿教</span><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（５）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">圆教。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l4 level1 lfo36"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">以上判教虽有不同，但顿渐二教，诸家同摄。《净影疏》曰：‘今此经者，为根熟人顿教法门。云何知顿。此经正为凡夫人中厌畏生死，求正定者，教令发心，生于净土。不从小故大，故知是顿。’大佑判小本曰：‘渐顿分之，此属顿教。’我国诸师判本经大小二本，多同此说，为顿教所摄。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l4 level1 lfo36"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">若依五教，古今中外净宗大德，莫不以本经实属圆教。蕅益指出，天台纯圆，贤首全圆，其秘髓与奥藏，皆不出此大经之外。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l4 level1 lfo36"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(五)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《圆中钞》云：‘圆顿法门，亦必须先开圆解，而次修圆行，破三惑而证圆果，以阶不退。今则但说彼土依正二报，以之为开解生信。七日持名，一心不乱。以之为造修行门。临命终时，即得往生，以阶跋致。……故知此经为五浊恶世之无上醍醐。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l4 level1 lfo36"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(六)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《甄解》称本经为‘专中之专，顿中之顿，真中之真，圆中之圆。’‘绝对不二之教，一乘真如之道也。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:1.0cm;text-indent:0cm;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">五、印光大师赞《要解》云：‘理事各臻其极。为自佛说此经以来第一注解，妙极确极。纵令古佛出世，重注此经，亦不能高出其上矣。’可见上之判教，如佛亲判也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">六、《疏钞》广引净宗经中事事无碍之文，深意苦心，以证本经正显事事无碍法界，亦即证明本经属于圆教，实不容或疑也。黄念祖居士依《疏钞》义，专论本经中事事无碍之相。取《探玄记》所说十玄次第，引证经文，以明本经实具十玄，于是确证本经正属圆教，即是中本《华严》。以证如上之说，皆是真实语，非因净宗行人之妄自尊大也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">七、《华严》末后以十大愿王导归极乐。今本经全显极乐，导引凡圣，同归极乐。故称本经为中本《华严》，诚有据也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Toc388334215"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334215"><a name="a07"></a><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334215"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">七、部类差别</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、同部：本经与小本《弥陀》，是同部经典。《疏钞》谓大小二本，‘文有繁简，义无胜劣’，判属同部。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="text-indent:-1.35pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">小本现有三种。秦唐二译，大体相同，稍有出入。秦译精要流畅，众所宗向。唐译则准确详备，力保原面。夏莲居居士撷其精要，更据六朝石经补足秦译所佚之廿一字。按襄阳石经乃六朝人手书而刻石者，于‘一心不乱’之下，尚有‘专持名号，以称名故，诸罪消灭，即是多善根福德因缘’。会本依古德意，补入此廿一字，大有益于圣教。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="text-indent:-1.35pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="margin-left:28.5pt;text-indent:-28.5pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:l8 level1 lfo37"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:书法家简中楷体;mso-font-width:100%">二、</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">同类：凡倡导忆念弥陀，愿生极乐之经典，均属同类。其中亦有亲疏，以持名念佛为亲，其他为疏。如：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="margin-left:28.5pt;text-indent:-1.5pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（一）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《佛说观无量寿佛经》。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="margin-left:28.5pt;text-indent:-1.5pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（二）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《华严经普贤行愿品》。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="margin-left:28.5pt;text-indent:-1.5pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（三）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《楞严经大势至菩萨念佛圆通章》。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="margin-left:28.5pt;text-indent:-1.5pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（四）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《往生论》。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="margin-left:28.5pt;text-indent:-1.5pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（五）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《安乐集》等等。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="margin-left:28.5pt;text-indent:-1.5pt;line-height:normal;mso-list:skip"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三、带说净土经论：《净宗经论合刊》内载有二百四十余种。此乃略举其要，遗漏难免。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Toc388334216"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334216"><a name="a08"></a><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334216"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">八、译会校释</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、‘译’指本经之原译本。‘会校’指根据诸种原译而会集校订之本。‘释’指本经诸本之注释。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、本经译本，自汉迄宋，凡有十二。宋元而降，仅存五种。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l3 level1 lfo38"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《无量清净平等觉经》。后汉月氏沙门支娄迦谶译于洛阳。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l3 level1 lfo38"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《佛说诸佛阿弥陀三耶三佛萨楼佛檀过度人道经》。一名《无量寿经》。一名《阿弥陀经》。吴月氏优婆塞支谦译。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l3 level1 lfo38"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">《无量寿经》。曹魏印度沙门康僧铠译于洛阳白马寺。嘉平四年壬申（公元二五二年）<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334217"><a name="a09"></a><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">◎</span><span style="color:#333399"> </span></span><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc388334217"><span style="font-family:新细明体;mso-ascii-color:#333399">九、总释名题</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="color:#333399"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">一、凡经之名题，皆直显本经之要旨，使学者因名达体，一览经题，直窥全经大义。是以天台家注经，先释经名。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">二、正释本经经题：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l25 level1 lfo43"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">‘佛’：义为觉者。自觉、觉他、觉满。即本师释迦牟尼佛，暨十方三世一切诸佛。斯义深广无尽。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l25 level1 lfo43"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">说：悦也。悦所怀也。佛以度生为愿，今众生机熟，堪受净土大法，究竟解脱。故今乐说，以畅本怀。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l25 level1 lfo43"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">大乘：喻也。乘以运载为义。此经所示，能念之心，本来是佛，是能乘大。起心念佛，念念作佛，故所乘者大。强名大乘。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l25 level1 lfo43"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">无量寿：梵语阿弥陀。阿弥陀三字是密语，含无量义。今翻无量寿，是取其中主要一义。是此德号，明法藏因地愿行，与果地佛德，极乐之依正主伴。一部所说，摄无不尽也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l25 level1 lfo43"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(五)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">庄严：经云：‘一向专志庄严妙土’，‘庄严众行’，又‘我已成就庄严佛土，清净之行’，‘彼极乐界，无量功德，具足庄严’。正显事事无碍法界之圆明具德也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l25 level1 lfo43"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(六)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">清净：身口意三业，离一切恶行烦染垢染之谓。经云：‘心净则佛土净。’表一切即一，一即一切。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l25 level1 lfo43"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(七)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">平等觉：经云：‘平等者，名为真如。乃至平等者，即不二法门。’今题中‘平等觉’有四解：<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo44"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">平等普觉一切众生。即一切众生平等成佛之法。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo44"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">以平等法觉悟众生。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo44"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">平等觉指如来之正觉。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo44"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">４、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">专就本经‘平等觉’，亦即极乐教主之圣号。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l48 level1 lfo45"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(八)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">经：义为契经。</span><span class="a0"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（１）</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">契理</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;letter-spacing:0pt;mso-font-width:100%">（２）</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">契机。具贯摄常法四义。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l47 level1 lfo46"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">１、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">贯则贯穿所说之理。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l47 level1 lfo46"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">２、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">摄则摄持所化之生。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l47 level1 lfo46"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">３、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">常则古今不易。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l47 level1 lfo46"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">４、<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">法则近远同遵。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="3" style="margin-left:53.85pt;text-indent:0cm;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">三、七种立题中，本经属具足立题。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l15 level1 lfo47"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">佛—此土果人。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l15 level1 lfo47"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">无量寿—彼土果人。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l15 level1 lfo47"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">庄严清净平等觉—是法。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l15 level1 lfo47"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(四)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">大乘—是喻。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:1.0cm;text-indent:0cm;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">四、诸经皆具教行理。本理立教，依教修行，从行显理。故须明本经题中别具之教行理。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l42 level1 lfo48"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(一)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">佛说大乘—是教。依无量寿之理体，宣众生本具之大经。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l42 level1 lfo48"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(二)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">庄严清净平等觉—是行。一句弥陀，庄严自心，清净自心。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="line-height:normal;mso-list:l42 level1 lfo48"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:文新字海-中楷;mso-font-width:100%">(三)<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> </span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">无量寿—是理。表三际一如、涅槃常住之法身本体，亦即众生本具之佛性，是心是佛之本心。实际理体也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="20" style="margin-left:1.0cm;text-indent:0cm;line-height:normal;mso-list:none"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">五、以经题配三觉：无量寿佛，是我本觉。发菩提心，一向专念，以庄严自心，是名始觉。始觉合本觉，直趋究竟觉，即是题中‘清净平等觉’也。<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="a" style="line-height:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;mso-font-width:100%">六、又可解为：‘平等觉’是我本觉。起心念佛，以自‘庄严’，是为始觉。始本不离，直趋觉路，顿入寂光，证‘无量寿’。如是则平等觉是我因心，无量寿是我果觉。妙义辗转无尽，悉在一题之中。古云：‘闻首题名字，获无量功德。’<span lang="EN-US"></span></span></p>
