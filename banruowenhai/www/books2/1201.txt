<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">（匆促写录、未及详考、或有讹误、俟后改正。又分类编辑、亦多未妥。俟后整理重辑。第二次草稿。己卯十二月二十二日录竟苟装、时居蓬峰掩室习律、年六十。弘一）<span lang="EN-US">   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt;color:#333399">诸法五位<span lang="EN-US">   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>一小乘俱舍宗<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>┌一色法<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>有物质之形者、又以物质为因而生者。  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">佛家建立诸法┌立七十五法<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>┐<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>│二心法<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>了识事物者。<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>亦名心王。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">有三门───┼二小乘成实宗 ├总以之收┼三心所法<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>随附于心法而起者。<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>是为心法所有之法、故名心所法。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>│立八十四法<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>│束于五位│四不相应法<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>不附随于心法者。  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>└三大乘法相宗 ┘<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>└五无为法<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>常住、而不自因缘生者。  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>立百法  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">◎ 小乘成实宗八十四法。无一一记载之明文。今略。<span lang="EN-US">   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">◎ 小乘俱舍宗、为物心两实之宗。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>◎大乘法相宗、为唯心无心无物之宗。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">故其次第为色、心、心所、不相应、无为。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>故其次第为心、心所、色、不相应、无为。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">于此中摄七十五法。┬色法………十一┐<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>于此中收百法。┬心法……………八┐   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>├心法…………一│<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>├心所法……五十一│  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>├心所法…四十六├七十五法<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>├色法…………十一├百法  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>├不相应法…十四│<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>├不相应法…二十四│  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>└无为法………三┘<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>└无为法…………六┘  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">┌色法<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>有质碍、而无知觉（缘虑）之用。<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>┐   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">│<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>├在于诸法、谓之色心。在于有情、谓之   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">└心王（心法）<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>无质碍、而有知觉之用。或为缘起诸法之根本。┘身心、身即色也。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">┌心王（心法）<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>为心之主作用。<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>总了别所对之境。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">└心所<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>为心之伴作用。<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>为心王之所有、而起贪嗔等之别作用也。<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>旧译曰心数。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>┌一所依平等<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>谓心王依眼根、则心所亦依眼根也。  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">┌心王（心法）┐<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>同时而起之一聚心<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>├二所缘平等<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>谓心王缘青境、则心所亦缘青境也。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>

<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>└五事平等<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>谓心王其体为一个、则心所之体亦各为一个也。  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><a name="a02"></a><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt;color:#333399">三有为法<span lang="EN-US">   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>

<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>┌小乘俱舍<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>受戒时、以强盛之身口表业为缘、满身四大制造之一种色体也。此色体有防非止功  <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">┌因生假<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>一切有为法、由因而生、皆为无性。<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>如六因四缘生诸法。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>

<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">观我法俱空、故超越于萨婆多宗之我空法有观。但不知空即不空、故称之为偏宛观。<span lang="EN-US">   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>

<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>└唯识圆教<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>“见大乘深教万法唯识之圆理者。阿含经所说小乘之观法也。”  <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p style="text-indent:18.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:9.0pt"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>├相空观<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>“观诸法之相为空也。若许因缘生之相为实有、犹是妄见。此更进而空无诸法之相、故  <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><a name="a19"></a><span style="color:#333399">日中考<span lang="EN-US">   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span style="color:#333399">持非时食戒者应注意日中之时<span lang="EN-US">   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">比丘戒中有非时食戒。八关斋戒中亦有之。日中以后即不可食。又依僧祇律日正中时、名曰时非时。若食亦得轻罪。故知进食必在日中以前也。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">日中之时、俗称曰正午。常人每用日晷仪置于日光之下、俟日晷仪标影恰至正午。即谓是为日中之时。因即校正钟表以此时为十二点钟也。然以此方法常常核对、则发见可怀疑者二事。一者虽自置极精良正确之钟表。常尽与日晷仪核对。其正午之时每与日晷仪参差少许、不能符合。二者各都市城邑之标准时钟如上海江海关大自钟等、其正午之时亦每见其或迟或早茫无一定也。今说明其理如下。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">依近代天文学者言。普通纪日之法皆用太阳。而地球轨道原非平圆。故日之视行有盈缩。而太阳日之长<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>短亦因是骖差不齐。泰西历家以其不便于用。爰假设一太阳。即用真太阳中天时则谓之视午。就平午太阳。平太阳中天时谓之平午。校对钟表者即以此时为十二点钟。若真太阳中天时则谓之视午。就平午与视午相合或相差者大约言之。每年之中、唯有四天、平午与视午大致相合。余均有差。相差最多者、平午比视午或早十五分、或迟十六分。其每日相差之详细分秒、皆载在吾国教育部中央观象台所颁发之历书中。</span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">若能了解以上之义。于昔所怀疑者自能袪释。因钟表每日有固定同一之迟速、决不许其参差。而真太阳日之长短则参差不齐。故不能以真太阳之视午而校正钟表恒定为十二点钟也。其各都市城邑之标准时钟皆据平午、以教育部历书核对即可了然。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">吾人持非时食戒者、当依真太阳之视午、而定日中食时之标准。决不可误据平午而过时也。至于如何校正钟表可各任自意。或依平午者、宜购求教育部历书核对、即或知每日视午之时。若如是者、倘自置精良正确之钟表、则可不必常常校正拨动。否则仍依旧法以日晷保护政策之正午而校正钟表、恒定是为十二点钟。此亦无妨。但须常常核对日晷仪、常常拨动钟表时针。因如前所说真太阳日之长短参差不齐。未能如钟表每日有固定同一之迟速也。又近代天文学者以种种之理由而斥日晷仪所测得者未能十分正确。此说固是。但其舛甚微无足计也。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><a name="a20"></a><span style="color:#333399">周尺考<span lang="EN-US">   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">南山律尺量皆用周尺。今考周尺一尺二寸当唐尺一尺。事钞谓唐朝用尺、五种不同。必以姬周尺秤以定。官市衡量、无事不平。资持记释云、五种者、旧云南吴尺、短周二寸。姬周尺十寸为定。唐尺、加周二寸、尺二为尺。山东尺、加唐二寸、尺四为尺。潞州罗柯尺。加山东二寸、尺六为尺。国家不禁、致此多别。至于公用、还准周尺。参看资持记第十册第十九卷二十一页及下附古今尺略图并周尺别形。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">（略）<span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Courier New&quot;">  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><a name="a21"></a><span style="color:#333399">受十善戒法<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>南山三大部中不载。唯丙山晚年所撰释门归苟仪中、略明。   <o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">归苟仪云受十善法者、谓身三口四意三善行。此之十业、戒善之宗。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>今多依相、罕有受者。今谓不然。先须愿祈不造众恶、依愿起行、有可承准。若不预作、辄然起善、内无轨辖、后遇罪缘、便造不止。由先无愿、故造众恶。大圣知机、故令受善。</span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">又云。次明受法。有师从受、无师自誓。如上三归已、口自发言、我某甲尽形寿于一切众生起慈仁意、不起杀心。如后九善例此、而不复繁文。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">案受十善戒者、别有受十善戒经委明。今未及检寻、且依归苟仪文酌定受法如下。其示相文、依灵峰选佛谱十善文录写。可暂承用。俟后检寻受十善戒经、再为改订可耳。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。归依佛、归依法、归依僧、尽形寿、受持十善戒法。三说</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲归依佛竟、归依法竟、归依僧竟、尽形寿受持十善戒法竟。三结</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。救护生命、不杀生。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。给施资财、不偷盗。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。遵修梵行、不淫欲。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>若在家人、改为不邪淫。</span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。和合彼此、不两舌。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。善言安慰、不恶口。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。作利益语、不绮语。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。常怀舍心、不悭贪。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。恒生慈愍、不嗔恚。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">我某甲。尽形寿。正信因果、不邪见。</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">已上各一说</p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">回向。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span>如常可知。</span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">  <!--[if !supportEmptyParas]-->&nbsp;  <!--[endif]-->  <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;mso-char-indent-size:12.0pt">南山律谓、意三者、大乘初念即犯、成宗次念乃犯。次念者、所谓重缘思觉、即是后念还追前事也。今初心受持者、宜先依成宗次念之例行之。</p>
